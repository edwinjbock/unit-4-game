The world is full of people trying to be someone else. Thus, we have cosplayers. Do you have what it takes to join in a melee with some of these elite, imitation warriors?

The goal is to use the attacks on the above-right to reach the maximum damage possible WITHOUT KILLING THEM. If you go OVER your cosplay opponent's Hit Points then you will have murdered a silly, costumed civilian. If you go UNDER then you lose. Oh, and the attack values change with every match, so good luck.

Your opponent will be chosen at random for each match.
